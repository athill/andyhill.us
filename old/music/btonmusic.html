One of my favorite things to to is to go see local and independent bands. I used to go to almost every show I could, though age and finances prevent me from being as active now as I was then. The main place I go see shows is Second Story(website by Andrew Storms). If you ever come to Bloomington and it's still open(which is in doubt right now) it's where I'd go. The other hot spot(which is sadly closed now) was the Cellar Lounge. New venues I haven't checked out include Vertigo, Space 101, and Uncle Festers. All-age venues include Rhino's and the John Waldron Arts Center. At any of these venues, you can find everything from bluegrass to hard-core, hip-hop to garage punk.
