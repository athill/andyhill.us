Tortilla-Black Bean Casserole
Mexican

Onion, diced
1
Green Peppers, diced
1
Stewed tomatoes
14 oz. can
Garlic
1/2 t.
Cumin
2 t.
Black beans, drained
32 oz.
Corn tortillas
12-16
Cheddar
1 1/2 c.

In a large skillet over medium heat, combine first 6 ingredients, bringing to a boil.
Reduce heat and simmer for 5 minutes.
Stir in beans.
Oil 9x13 casserole dish and spread 1/3 of the bean mixture in.
Cover with tortillas, then 2/5 of the cheese.
Repeat steps 4 and 5.
Add remaining bean mixture and cheese.
Cover and bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.
